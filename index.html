About Our Company

Jayamali cane craft is a Sri Lankan manufacturer of cane handcrafted products. We are traditional cane craftsmen with the years of experience in furniture industry which has being supplied to many hotels & homes in Sri Lanka. With an aptitude innovative, inborn & strong passion for design business, Founded by Mr. Sidney de Zoysa in 1989 as a small business. We always try to use sustainable material such as rattan, cane, bamboo, reed and other natural materials for our products to decorate your home, office, hotel and restaurants with the years of durability and fabulous look. For the creativity and the design talent of our staff, our work is rewarded by Sri Lanka governmet by winning presidential awards in 1995,1998,1999,2002,2003,2004,2011,2014 and 2015.

Mistakenly some people confuse furniture or chair caning with wicker. To clarify, chair caning is specifically the craft of applying rattan cane or rattan peel to a piece of furniture such as the backs or seats of chairs, whereas wicker or wicker work is a reference to the craft of weaving any number of materials such as willow or rattan reeds as well as man-made paper based cords.
